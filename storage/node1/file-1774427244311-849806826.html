Menu Section

🍗 Chicken Biryani

A classic Indian rice dish cooked with aromatic basmati rice. Prepared using tender chicken pieces marinated in rich spices. Slow-cooked on a dum flame for deep and authentic flavor. Perfect balance of spices, herbs, and saffron aroma. Served hot with raita and salan on the side. A favorite dish loved by all age groups.

🥘 Paneer Butter Masala

Soft paneer cubes cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy. Flavored with butter, fresh cream, and mild Indian spices. Rich texture with a smooth and slightly sweet taste. Best enjoyed with butter naan or roti. Perfect choice for vegetarians and paneer lovers. A royal North Indian delicacy.

🫓 Butter Naan

Freshly prepared soft Indian bread baked in a tandoor. Brushed with pure butter for rich taste and aroma. Light, fluffy, and easy to tear. Pairs perfectly with curries and gravies. Served hot for the best dining experience. A must-have with North Indian dishes.

🍛 Veg Meals

A complete traditional South Indian vegetarian meal. Includes rice, sambar, rasam, curry, and curd. Prepared using fresh seasonal vegetables. Balanced flavors with authentic home-style cooking. Healthy, filling, and comforting. Served on a banana leaf or plate.

🍖 Mutton Curry

Slow-cooked mutton pieces in a spicy onion-based gravy. Marinated with traditional Indian spices for rich flavor. Tender meat that melts in the mouth. Perfect blend of heat and aroma. Best enjoyed with rice or roti. A favorite for non-vegetarian food lovers.

🍨 Gulab Jamun

Soft milk-based dumplings soaked in sugar syrup. Flavored with cardamom for a sweet aroma. Warm, juicy, and delicious dessert. Perfect ending to a hearty meal. Loved by both kids and adults. A classic Indian sweet treat.
